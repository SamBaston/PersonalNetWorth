WEALTHSPHERE EXPANDED FEATURES LIST
=================================

In addition to the requested features, the following enhancements have been implemented to provide a professional-grade financial auditing experience:

1. FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY SCORE (DEBT-TO-EQUITY)
   - Calculated as 100 - (Debt-to-Equity * 50), capped at 0-100.
   - Provides an immediate pulse on financial leverage.

2. ASSET MIX DISTRIBUTION PIE CHART
   - Real-time breakdown of Cash vs Investments vs Fixed Assets.
   - Helps in visualizing portfolio diversification.

3. PREDICTIVE NET WORTH TRAJECTORY
   - Area chart showing historical data (solid) vs future predictions (dotted).
   - Utilizes estimated return rates (7% default for stocks, 2% for cash) and actual liability interest rates.

4. INDIVIDUAL COMPONENT PROJECTIONS
   - Users can click on any asset or liability to see its specific future value growth or debt decay.
   - Differentiated lines (dotted for predictions).

5. STRATEGY INSIGHTS (AI-STYLE ANALYTICS)
   - Dynamic footer cards that analyze current leverage and predict goal completion dates.
   - Tailored advice based on student loan plan interest vs investment returns.

6. CURRENCY NORMALIZATION (GBP/USD)
   - Automatic conversion for USD assets using real-time FX estimates (0.79 factor) for accurate total net-worth calculation.

7. AUTOMATIC DAILY INTEREST ACCRUAL
   - Liabilities automatically calculate interest accrued since the last update, ensuring the "Snapshot" is always accurate down to the day.

8. RESPONSIVE DASHBOARD LAYOUT
   - Modern, high-density interface designed for both long-term tracking and quick mobile check-ins.
